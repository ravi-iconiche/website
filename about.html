About Iconiche

Incorporated in 2012, Iconiche Learning is a young, result-oriented company focused on providing cutting edge learning solutions to some of the top corporate houses that have an eye on human asset development. The Vision of Iconiche is to be Iconically Niche in Learning and Consulting.
Our Strength emanates from 50+ years of cross-functional experience the founders have in the field of Business, Technology, Communication and Education. Add to this the wealth of skills we have in Instructional Design, Delivery and Learning Management and Learning Consultants. Iconiche has been successfully delivering inspired learning experience to large organizations in Telecom, IT, Manufacturing, FMCG and Education. We come with an enviable domain expertise in Telecom and hence most of our functional programs are in that space. Participants immensely benefit from our programs as it employs time-tested workshop style learning techniques blended with role-plays and live case studies. At the end of each program, participants are able to apply their learning in their roles and see measurable improvements.

Our Differentiators

At Iconiche, we approach your needs with a single objective – results.

  • We have a strategic focus on long-term relationships and results
  • We offer completely customized training programs that meet key objectives
  • We design best in class Training Content that brings out the value of the program
  • We are outcome focused
  • We keep things practical and simple
  • Our Learning Consultants come with rich and relevant experience

Our Process

We use a relatively simple end-to-end process to develop our training programs. The key to the success of this approach is that we collaborate with our clients at each and every step to ensure that the training program will be well received by the intended audience and will meet / exceed the organization’s required outcomes.

Ravi Chandran - Chief Executive Officer

Ravi Chandran is a Senior Learning Consultant specializing in areas of Sales, Leadership and Telecom. He comes with a rich 34 years of cross-functional experience in some of the top-notch organizations.

Ravi had held executive positions in Corporates like Bharti Airtel, Reliance Communications, Ingram Micro and Fujitsu. He is an industry veteran in B2B Selling, Business Development, Strategy and Consultative Selling. He has to his credit consulting experience with some of the global Organizations at CxO levels.

A post-graduate in Operations Research from National Institute of Technology, Ravi is certified on Strategic Selling and Leadership Coaching from ISB, IIM, Blanchard International and Meynard Leigh. He is a Six Sigma Green Belt trained and has experience in rolling out Balance Score Card based performance management system. He is often invited as speaker at industry forums and been a visiting faculty to some of the leading B-Schools like Symbiosis. He has conducted experiential workshops at Bharti Airtel, Reliance, Nokia, GE, Molex, Intel, L&T and many more.

At Iconiche, Ravi handles the Business Development, Strategy and Consulting.

Jayanthi. R - Chief Operating Officer & Principal Learning Consultant

Jayanthi is Senior Learning Consultant with over two decades of training experience.

Jayanthi began her training career as a Corporate Trainer with Aptech in 1993. She has been a Communicative Language Trainer (CLT) since February 2003 after undergoing an in-depth workshop on CLT and Presentation Skill from British Council. She is an experienced Train-the-Trainer in Voice and Accent.

Jayanthi is a practitioner of NLP (Neuro Linguistic Programming). As a seasoned corporate trainer, she is engaged with some of the iconic organisations like Wipro, Nokia, Ciber, Ericsson, Infineon, Theorem, CrossDomain, Trelleborg. Jayanthi is a visiting faculty to some of the top B-Schools like Jain Group of Instuitions and IIFM. She has groomed many students to a successful corporate career moving from campuses.

Jayanthi specializes in Business Communications, Business Etiquette, Team Building, Presentation Skills, Time Management and other key people skills programs.

At Iconiche, Jayanthi handles the operations, program management and research.
