Iconiche Telecom Academy

Today, we live in an increasingly connected world. With Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and markets evolving rapidly, keeping pace requires a continuous learning process. Learning opportunities enables people to develop the right competencies, in the right place and at the right time making it critical to success/

A successful high-impact learning culture is one in which the value of learning is recognized, and where learning is seen as a critical investment and enabler for business success. It is one where employees feel empowered and responsible for driving their own professional development. Successful learning cultures foster an environment where everyday learning, informal and on-the-job learning as well as knowledge sharing form an integral part of the overall learning culture.
